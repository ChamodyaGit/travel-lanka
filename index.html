Nestled within the historic walls of the former Bogambara Prison in Kandy, Prison Park Restaurant by Laksam offers a truly one-of-a-kind dining experience. Surrounded by lush greenery and rich heritage, this venue blends the past and present into a serene escape with a distinctive ambiance.

At Prison Park, youll delight in a diverse menu featuring fresh, flavorful Sri Lankan favoriteslike the renowned chicken kottuserved in earthenware that enhances the dining experience. Generous portions, great value, and attentive service make it a standout choice for both lunch and dinner.

Dont miss the weekend buffeta feast of authentic Sri Lankan rice and curry, available every Friday through Sunday from 11:30 AM to 3:00 PM. This Rice & Curry Feast is a favorite among locals and visitors alike, offering a flavorful showcase of Sri Lankas culinary traditions.
